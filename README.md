# Budget Calculator | حاسبة الميزانية

A simple, bilingual (English / Arabic) budget calculator that runs entirely in your browser. Track your income and expenses, see your net income and savings rate live, and view a clear breakdown of where your money goes.

**Live demo:** https://zoldyeq.github.io/Fahd-s-BudgetCalculator/

---

## Features

- **Income tracking** — Basic, secondary, and investment income, plus custom sources you can name, add, or remove.
- **Expense tracking** — Rent, food, utilities, petrol, debts, investment, and custom categories.
- **Live donut charts** — Color-coded breakdown of both income and expenses, each with a legend and percentage share.
- **Net income & savings rate** — Instantly see what's left and what percentage of your income you're keeping.
- **Monthly / Annual toggle** — View any figure as a monthly amount or multiplied across the full year.
- **English / Arabic** — Switch languages anytime; the interface flips fully, including right-to-left layout.
- **Private by design** — Everything runs in your browser. No data is saved, sent, or shared.

## How to use

Open the live demo, enter your income and expense amounts, and the totals, percentages, and charts update as you type. No sign-up, no install.

## Built with

Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies. Hosted free on GitHub Pages.
